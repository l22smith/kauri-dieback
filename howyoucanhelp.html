How you can help

Spread Awareness

One of the most important ways you can help is by spreading awareness about Kauri Dieback. Share information about the disease with your friends, family, and community. Educate others about the importance of protecting kauri trees and the role they play in the ecosystem.

Practice Good Hygiene

When visiting kauri forests, always use cleaning stations provided to wash your footwear and equipment. Make sure to clean off all soil and organic matter to prevent the spread of the disease. Stick to designated tracks and avoid stepping on kauri roots to minimize the risk of transmitting the fungus.

Support Conservation Efforts

You can support organizations and initiatives that are working to protect kauri trees and manage Kauri Dieback. Consider volunteering your time, donating to conservation projects, or participating in community events aimed at raising funds and awareness for the cause.

Get Involved in Research

Scientists and researchers are continuously studying Kauri Dieback to better understand its causes and develop effective strategies for management and prevention. You can contribute to these efforts by participating in citizen science projects, reporting sightings of infected trees, and supporting research initiatives.
